# Naming — the official model (locked 2026-06-26)

One name, one job. Use these consistently; stop using any name for more than one thing.

| Name | What it is | Audience | Protect as |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Infinite Pieces AI** | The company | Public | Trademark (Class 9 + 42) |
| **Infinite Suite OS™** | The product clinics license (software running in their clinic) | Public / customers | Trademark (Class 9 + 42) |
| **Recovery Radar™** | The **owner-facing dashboard** — a clinic CEO logs in and sees how well the Recovery Waterfall is recovering hours across their sites (recovery rate, hours/$ recovered, which learners/sites recover most/least, at-risk alerts) | Customers (clinic owners) | Trademark (Class 9 + 42) |
| **Recovery Waterfall™** | The **system/method** — the flow that turns a cancellation/callout into a recovered hour (cancel → Scheduler AI → auth check → SubPool → telehealth → makeup → admin) | Customers (as a named method) | Trademark the **name**; keep the **algorithm** a trade secret |
| **SubPool™** | Substitute/coverage marketplace inside Infinite Suite OS | Customers | Trademark |
| **FieldPocket™ / Care Pocket™ / Analyst Pocket™** | Role-facing app family | Customers | Consider a **"Pocket"** family mark |
| **(internal lead-gen suite)** | Your founder workspace — scans the market to find clinics to sell to (Lead Machine, EMR Shopping Radar, Executive Prospector, Intelligence Bank, CRM, outreach, SEO Page Factory) | **Internal only** | **Trade secret** — needs its own quiet codename; do NOT brand publicly |

## Key decisions

- **Recovery Radar is now a customer product, not the secret internal tool.** The internal lead-gen suite needs a new internal codename (it was loosely called "Recovery Radar" before). Keep the internal suite behind auth and out of public copy.
- **Two mirror-image workspaces, two logins:**
  - *Founder workspace* (you) — scans the **market** (outward). Login: founders@infinitepieces.ai.
  - *Owner workspace* (clinic CEO) — scans **their own clinic** (inward) = Recovery Radar. Demo login: demo@infinitepieces / infinitedemo.
- **Trademark vs trade secret:** brand the public names (Infinite Pieces AI, Infinite Suite OS, Recovery Radar, Recovery Waterfall, SubPool, Pocket family). Keep the recovery *algorithm* and the internal lead-gen suite as trade secrets (NDAs, don't disclose).
