# LinkedIn Executive Prospector

This workspace feature helps find possible clinic executives, founders, owners, clinical directors and operations leaders from Google-indexed public LinkedIn profile results.

## What it does

- Accepts company/niche keywords such as `ABA clinic`, `ABA therapy`, `behavior analyst clinic`, or a specific clinic name.
- Accepts a market/location such as Florida, Tampa, Texas, or United States.
- Accepts target titles such as CEO, Founder, Owner, Clinical Director, Operations Manager, Practice Administrator.
- Searches public Google-indexed LinkedIn profile snippets through SerpApi.
- Extracts likely name, title, company, LinkedIn URL, keyword, location and confidence score.
- Exports CSV or Google Sheets-ready clipboard rows.
- Copies names for manual LinkedIn search.

## What it does not do

- It does not log into LinkedIn.
- It does not scrape private LinkedIn pages.
- It does not scrape private groups or private messages.
- It does not auto-send LinkedIn messages.
- It does not bypass platform limits or access controls.

## Recommended workflow

1. Search a niche/market.
2. Export or copy names.
3. Open each LinkedIn profile manually.
4. Confirm the person is actually a relevant executive or decision maker.
5. Add only reviewed business-level information to CRM.
6. Draft a human-reviewed LinkedIn note or email.
