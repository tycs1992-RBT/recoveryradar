# Compliance and Risk Guardrails

Recovery Radar is not a PHI collection system. Public calculator, quiz, and chatbot tools should store only clinic-level counts, rates, and workflow descriptions.

## Do not collect

- patient names
- dates of birth
- medical record numbers
- insurance IDs
- treatment notes
- patient addresses
- caregiver-specific clinical details

## Use language like

- privacy-aware
- HIPAA-conscious
- role-based access
- consent-aware workflows
- audit-readiness

## Do not say

- guaranteed HIPAA compliant
- guaranteed payer approved
- guaranteed billing compliant

AI content is draft assistance only. Humans should review outreach before sending. Do not misrepresent identity, relationships, or clinic results.
