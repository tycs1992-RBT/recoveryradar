# Intelligence Bank

The Intelligence Bank is the workspace's deduped master store for gathered public business information.

## Goals

- Keep every business, clinic, company, executive and source link in one place.
- Keep results alphabetized by name.
- Avoid duplicate companies, people, emails, websites and profile links.
- Let Lead Machine and Executive Prospector save selected results into the same bank.

## Deduping rules

The workspace checks records by:

- Website/domain
- Public email
- LinkedIn/profile URL
- Business/person name plus company/location

## Current sources

- Lead Machine
- Executive Prospector
- Existing CRM leads
- Existing company/contact tables

## Workflow

1. Search in Lead Machine or Executive Prospector.
2. Review selected records.
3. Click Save to Bank.
4. Open Intelligence Bank.
5. Refresh and export the deduped alphabetized master list.

## Important guardrails

Use only public business-level information. Do not store PHI, private messages, private group details, or unverified personal details. Manual review is required before outreach.
