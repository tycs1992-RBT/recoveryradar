# Pricing and Offer

Because Infinite Suite OS™ is an operational recovery overlay, not a full EMR replacement on day one, Phase 1 pricing should be lower-friction than full EMR pricing.

## Do not charge per staff user

ABA clinics have staff turnover, part-time RBTs, caregivers, BCBAs, schedulers, admins, substitutes, supervisors, and floaters. Staff-seat pricing creates friction.

Use:

- Per active learner
- Unlimited staff seats
- Unlimited caregiver seats

## Design Partner Beta

- 1–2 clinics only
- 90 days free
- structured feedback required
- monthly scorecard required
- permission to use anonymized outcomes if successful

## Founding Clinic Trial

- $15 per active learner/month
- $500/month minimum
- unlimited staff seats
- unlimited caregiver seats
- no implementation fee for founding cohort
- 3-month pilot
- recovered-hour scorecard included

## Standard Phase 1

- $20 per active learner/month
- $750/month minimum
- unlimited staff seats
- unlimited caregiver seats
- optional implementation fee later

## Sales math

3 recovered sessions/month × 3 hours/session = 9 recovered hours/month.

At $80/hour, that is $720/month recovered value.

A $500–$750/month product can justify itself if it recovers only a few missed sessions.
