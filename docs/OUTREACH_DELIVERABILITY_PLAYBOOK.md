# Outreach Deliverability Playbook

Goal: send more outreach without burning the Infinite Pieces AI domain reputation or creating compliance risk.

## Competitive edge features to build

1. Lead source proof

Every outreach lead should keep a source URL, source type, search query, company guess, website, phone, email/form source, and manual-review status.

2. Human approval queue

Do not auto-send newly discovered leads. Move each lead through: discovered -> reviewed -> approved -> sequenced -> sent -> replied -> opted out.

3. Suppression list enforcement

Every send action must check a suppression list before sending. Suppressed contacts and domains should never be emailed again unless manually reactivated with a reason.

4. Unsubscribe footer

Every commercial email should include a clear opt-out line and the company postal address.

5. Sender authentication checklist

Before increasing volume, confirm SPF, DKIM, DMARC, custom tracking domain, Google Postmaster Tools, bounce domain, and reply-to address.

6. Rate-limited sending

Start low and grow slowly. Do not send in bursts. Suggested early-stage cadence:

- Week 1: 20-40/day
- Week 2: 40-75/day
- Week 3: 75-125/day
- Week 4: 125-200/day only if spam/bounce rates stay low

7. Personalization tokens

Use source-aware templates:

- clinic name
- state/city
- pain signal
- source category
- current likely EMR/stack signal
- calculator CTA
- provider portal CTA

8. Sequence builder

Recommended founder-led sequence:

- Day 0: short founder note
- Day 3: lost-hours calculator follow-up
- Day 7: provider portal walkthrough invite
- Day 14: final value note with no-pressure opt-out

9. Bounce and reply handling

Mark bounced emails, bad domains, generic inboxes and replies. Do not keep sending to addresses that bounce.

10. Channel mix

Use email plus LinkedIn/manual follow-up, not email blasts alone.

- Are cancellations costing your clinic hours?
- Before switching ABA software
- Recover missed ABA sessions without replacing your EMR

Short CTA:

Use the Lost Hours Calculator: https://www.infinitepieces.ai/calculator  
Then tour the Provider Portal: https://www.infinitepieces.ai/provider-portal

## Hard no list

Do not:

- use deceptive subject lines
- imply an existing relationship that does not exist
- scrape private groups or private messages
- collect PHI
- send to opt-outs
- hide unsubscribe language
- blast a new domain at high volume
- buy random email lists and send immediately
