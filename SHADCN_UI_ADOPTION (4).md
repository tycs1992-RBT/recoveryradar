# Recovery Radar™ 30-Day Launch Plan

## Week 1: Foundation

- Confirm product thesis and no-migration positioning.
- Set up repo, environment variables, database, and CI.
- Seed keyword groups and fictional sample leads.
- Verify calculator, quiz, and chatbot do not request PHI.
- Begin manual public-source lead research to test scoring assumptions.

## Week 2: Core conversion tools

- Finalize Lost Hours Calculator UX and API persistence.
- Finalize ABA Operations Stack Quiz segmentation.
- Publish calculator-focused landing page.
- Prepare first two SEO articles.
- Set up Google Ads keyword groups without launching high spend.

## Week 3: CRM and outreach readiness

- Wire CRM views to persisted leads.
- Load message templates.
- Validate templates for no-migration language, opt-out language, and HIPAA-conscious claims.
- Configure email domain authentication outside the app.
- Create manual outreach approval workflow.

## Week 4: Bot, analytics, and beta

- Embed Recovery Advisor on the demo site or staging site.
- Connect analytics events for calculator, quiz, chatbot, and provider portal clicks.
- Launch limited traffic to calculator and quiz pages.
- Invite founding clinics.
- Review early completions, lead scores, and outreach responses.

## Day-30 MVP definition

By day 30, the MVP should be able to capture leads, estimate lost hours, segment visitors, guide them with the Recovery Advisor bot, score leads, prepare compliant outreach drafts, and manage follow-up status in the CRM dashboard.
